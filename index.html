FAQs

  • How do you make sure businesses will pay investors?

    We vet businesses rigorously to verify that the owners are trustworthy. We also mandate joint checking accounts for transparency, enabling investors to track fund usage and revenue updates.

  • How is the money held?

    Investor funds are securely managed via escrow services, with additional transparency measures like joint checking accounts to ensure responsible fund management.

  • How much money do I need to invest?

    Investment opportunities cater to all, starting from $50 up to $2500. Accredited investors can invest more than $2500, ensuring accessibility for all interested in supporting businesses.This is due to current CF regulation laws in place in the United States.

  • What happens if a business doesn't pay?

    We prioritize transparency and collaboration. In case of non-payment, we guide investors through the process, exploring options collectively, including legal recourse if necessary, ensuring fairness and trust within our community.
